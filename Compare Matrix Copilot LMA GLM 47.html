Strategic Positioning Summary

Where Sublime Wins

  • Prototype → Pilot → Early Production workflows
  • High‑complexity, low‑volume medical components
  • R&D‑heavy customers needing iterative development
  • Companies frustrated with slow timelines from large suppliers
  • Buyers needing ultrafast laser precision without enterprise‑level overhead

Where Competitors Dominate

  • Resonetics, Norman Noble, Admedes: High‑volume, regulated, implant‑grade manufacturing & Large OEM relationships.
  • Lighteum, MicroCut: Small‑shop agility, but less depth in process engineering.
  • Symmetry/ArcRev, Lumenous: Broader manufacturing services, but less specialization.
