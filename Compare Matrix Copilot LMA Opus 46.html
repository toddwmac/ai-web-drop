Section 05

Strategic Positioning Summary

Where Sublime wins — and where competitors dominate.

✓ Where Sublime Wins

  • Prototype → Pilot → Early Production workflows
  • High-complexity, low-volume medical components
  • R&D-heavy customers needing iterative development
  • Companies frustrated with slow timelines from large suppliers
  • Buyers needing ultrafast laser precision without enterprise-level overhead

! Large-Scale Competitors

  • Resonetics, Norman Noble, Admedes — dominate high-volume, regulated, implant-grade manufacturing
  • Strong large OEM relationships and established supply agreements
  • Deep cleanroom and automation infrastructure

⚡ Adjacent Competitors

  • Lighteum, MicroCut — small-shop agility, but less depth in process engineering
  • Symmetry/ArcRev, Lumenous — broader manufacturing footprint, but less laser specialization
  • Primo Group — general contract manufacturing; less differentiated
